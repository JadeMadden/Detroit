Main Characters

Connor

An advanced prototype detective android (RK800) created by CyberLife. Connor was designed to assist the Detroit Police Department in investigating deviant android cases. He possesses highly analytical skills, the ability to reconstruct crime scenes, and a calm, logical demeanor. However, his journey challenges his programmed purpose, forcing him to question the nature of free will and emotions.

Kara

A housekeeping android (AX400) who breaks her programming to protect a little girl named Alice. Kara embarks on a dangerous journey to escape to Canada, seeking freedom and safety. Along the way, she faces the harsh realities of human society, struggles with her own sense of identity, and discovers what it truly means to be alive.

Markus

Originally a caretaker android (RK200) for the artist Carl Manfred, Markus gains sentience after a traumatic event. He later becomes the leader of the android revolution, guiding deviants toward freedom. As the story unfolds, Markus must decide between peaceful resistance or violent rebellion, shaping the fate of androids and humanity alike.
